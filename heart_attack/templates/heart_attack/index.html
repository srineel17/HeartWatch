About HeartWatch

HeartWatch is a platform which is build to test your heart and the risk which it is going through right now. It is easy to use and very simple to understand. It is meant to eliminate the cost of consulting a doctor just to evaluate the reports of your tests. Feel free to contact us in any circumstances regarding any misconception about the platform.
